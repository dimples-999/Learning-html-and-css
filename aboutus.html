About us

Welcome to Readelle — your personal gateway to the world of books.
We created this app to make reading more accessible, enjoyable, and meaningful for everyone.
Whether you're a casual reader, a passionate bookworm, or someone just getting started,
our goal is to help you discover, track, and share your reading journey.

Who We Are

're a team of book lovers, designers, and developers who believe that stories have the power to inspire, connect, and transform.
This app was born from our own need for a better way to manage our reading lives
— and we thought others might need it too.

Mission

To help readers find great books, stay organized, and connect with a global community of readers — all in one beautifully simple app.
